## 3. AI-generated content

NachoMUD's world is built procedurally by Large Language Models
(Llama models running on the server). Rooms, NPC dialogue, DM
narration, and adjudication results are produced by the LLM in
response to player input.

LLM output is unpredictable. It can be:

- **Violent** — combat is core to the game; descriptions of injury and
  death are common.
- **Profane** — characters may swear; NPC dialogue is not censored.
- **Adult in tone** — themes of grief, betrayal, addiction, and other
  adult subject matter may appear.
- **Surprising or weird** — the model occasionally produces strange,
  surreal, or off-tone content. We try to constrain this with system
  prompts and content rules but cannot eliminate it.

We do **not** intentionally generate sexual content, depictions of
abuse against minors, instructions for real-world harm, or content
targeting real individuals. If you encounter output that violates
those constraints, report it via <howdy@nacho.bot> and we'll
investigate and adjust.

You explicitly accept that LLM-generated text is part of the
experience and won't be censored further than what's already
constrained by the system prompts.
